CSS Theme Architecture

  • Role: Frontend, design
  • Tech: ASP, HTML/CSS/JS

I developed the CSS template system for skinning the EventBuilder engine to look like our clients' websites. This was back before SASS et. al., so basically I pulled the primary colors and styles from the client's site and manually applied them to the base stylesheets of EventBuilder. It usually took me a day or so to get the basic look-n-feel of their site applied to our app.
